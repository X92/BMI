About BMI

Welcome to the BMI Calculator. The Body Mass Index is a measure of relative weight based on the mass and height of an individual. A Healthy BMI ranges from 18.5 - 24.9

Although your range may possibly fall outside of the healthy range, The measurement does not take in to consideration age or muscle mass. Extremely fit athletes are known to be considered overweight
